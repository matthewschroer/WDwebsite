My name is Matthew Schroer. I am a mechanical engineering student at Iowa stateuniversity. I grew up in Lakeville Minnesota in a family of five with two younger brothers. Being the oldest child of the family I am the first of my brothers to do a lot of things. Like driving a car, having a job, and going to college. My first year of college has been one of the most challenging and fun times of my life, Second only to owning my first horse. I have noticed that owning a horse and attending college have a lot in common. First, the biggest similarity is time and money management. Owning a horse takes a lot of time and money to keep them healthy and happy, and it is the same for me going to college. Second, it takes a lot of time, patience, and stubbornness to achieve all of your goals. I have applied these skills to improve my college writing and communication skills. Writing is a skill that has never come easily to me and I have had to work very hard to achieve what I have. My goals for the future are to make writing a fun and constructive part of my day and maybe write a book about adventure one day.
